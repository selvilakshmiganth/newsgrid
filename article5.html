Rainbow Laptops

Written By Brax Kaur | 15 Jun, 2020

Rainbow colors are just so nice, so trying to put them on a laptop is simply natural! But obviously paining over your laptop is not very convenient, so the best thing many of our totally existing readers have suggested is to just put a rainbow colored light on top of your laptop. We have tried that and the results are (not) outsanding, as you can see in the image above! So next time you get the chance just buy a rainbow colored lamp and shine you laptop! But remember not to overheat it or not being able to properly see the screen, as those are usualy bad things.
